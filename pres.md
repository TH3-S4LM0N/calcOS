## Features
- Simple math with all 5 operators:
  - `+` Addition
  - `-` Subtraction
  - `*` Multiplication
  - `/` Division
  - `^` Powers
- Algorithms: 
  - `Q_rsqrt`: Quake fast inverse square root
  - `sqrt`: [Square rooting](#why-square-rooting-is-fake)
